Why Flow3

Estonia's only completely incision-free laser procedure

Unlike traditional LASIK and FemtoLasik procedures, Flow3 makes no incision in the cornea. The laser reshapes only the surface layer of the cornea, which means fewer risks and faster recovery.

Completely touch-free

The procedure is performed without a scalpel or mechanical contact. The laser works solely on the corneal surface — painless and safe.

Quick procedure

The entire procedure takes just a few minutes. You can move about immediately afterwards, with full visual acuity stabilising within 2–3 months.

Better night vision

Flow3 preserves corneal biomechanics more effectively than incision-based methods, resulting in more stable vision in low-light conditions.

Later need for reading glasses

Clinical studies show that the touch-free method preserves near vision for longer — the need for reading glasses is delayed by an average of 5 years.

Personalised treatment plan

Every patient's eyes are different. The 60-minute pre-procedure examination measures thousands of parameters, from which an individual treatment plan is prepared.

Less energy, better results

Flow3 uses 25–50% less laser energy than competing methods. Less energy means less tissue affected, faster recovery, and better outcomes.

Flow3 eye examination

What happens during the examination?

The 60-minute examination provides a complete picture of your eye health. An optometrist and ophthalmic surgeon document thousands of parameters and give you a clear answer on whether laser treatment is suitable.

01

Corneal diagnostics

Topography, tomography, thickness measurement, pupil size, tear film analysis, and dozens of additional measurements.

02

Retinal and fundus examinations

Nerve studies, visual acuity tests, visual field assessment, and fundus photography.

03

Surgeon consultation and treatment plan

The ophthalmic surgeon analyses the examination results, explains your options, and prepares an individual treatment plan where appropriate.

Frequently asked questions

Useful to know before deciding

Flow3 is completely painless. Anaesthetic drops are applied to the eye before the procedure. The laser works solely on the corneal surface without ever touching the eye. Patients report that the experience is surprisingly gentle.
Functional vision returns within the first few days — you can move about and return to work shortly after the procedure. Full visual acuity stabilises within 2–3 months.
Flow3 is suitable for those aged 18–45 with a prescription of up to −9 dioptres and stable vision for at least 12 months. Final suitability is always confirmed by the eye examination.
Flow3 is the only method that makes no incision in the cornea. LASIK creates a flap incision, whilst SMILE makes a 3 mm incision. Flow3 treats only the surface layer of the cornea, preserving the eye's natural structural integrity. The result is fewer risks, better night vision, and a later need for reading glasses.
The Flow3 laser procedure costs €2,990 and includes eye drops for up to 3 months, follow-up visits for 12 months, sunglasses, and a 7-year guarantee. The eye examination costs €69*. Finance is available. *The examination fee is deducted in full if you book a KSA service within 60 days.
During the first week, you should avoid rubbing your eyes, swimming, and visiting saunas. Oxygen-permeable contact lenses protect the eyes for 5–6 days. Screen use and light physical activity are permitted almost immediately.
